Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Quantum P1NG is built with privacy as a primary goal. Our architecture is based on the principle of data minimization.

1. Data Collection

  • No Personal Data: We do not require phone numbers, emails, names, or access to your contacts to function.
  • Anonymous Identifiers: The only identifier used is your mailbox hash (SHA-256), which cannot be traced back to your real identity.
  • Cryptographic Keys: RSA private keys are generated and stored exclusively on your device. Only your public key is transmitted to the server to enable message delivery.

2. Message Handling

  • End-to-End Encryption: Every message is encrypted on the sender's device and can only be decrypted by the recipient. The server acts only as a temporary "Dead Drop".
  • Zero-Knowledge: We have no technical means to read your messages or attachments.
  • Automatic Deletion: Messages are deleted from the server immediately after delivery to the recipient.

3. Connectivity and Logs

  • No Server Logs: Our server is configured not to log client IP addresses.
  • Tor Integration: We natively support connections via the Tor network to hide your geographical location and traffic origin.
  • Onion Address: Users can choose to use the .onion address to completely bypass standard web gateways.
